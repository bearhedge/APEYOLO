    - NOTE on delimiters - 
    ----------------------
Unix and bash use \ (backslash)
Powershell will utlize ` (backtick)
Command Prompt uses ^ (caret)


Replace {{BearerToken}} with the SSO Bearer Token retrieved from POST /gw/api/v1/sso-sessions. Do consider adding the "Verbose parameter" into your request (-v) to get more details back on any failed request(s)

PORTFOLIO ACCOUNTS:
-------------------

  https://api.ibkr.com/v1/api/portfolio/accounts ^
  -H "accept: application/json" ^
  -H "Authorization: Bearer {{BearerToken}}" ^
  -d ""
